Cookie Policy

At Sohan Soft Tech, we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and provide personalized services. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember user preferences, improve performance, and deliver a more personalized experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the website to function properly (e.g., login, security, payment processing). Without them, certain features may not work.

Performance & Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how visitors use our website (e.g., pages visited, time spent, error reports). This data allows us to improve user experience.

Functional Cookies

These remember your preferences (e.g., language, region, saved settings) for a more customized experience.

Advertising & Marketing Cookies

We may use cookies to deliver relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness. These may include third-party cookies (e.g., Google, Meta).

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are placed by trusted third-party providers, such as analytics tools (Google Analytics) or advertising platforms. These providers may collect information to deliver services on our behalf.

4. How You Can Manage Cookies

You can choose to accept, reject, or delete cookies by adjusting your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

For detailed instructions, visit your browser's help section:

  • Chrome: Manage cookies in Chrome
